Biodiesel Solutions

At Royal International, we specialize in producing biodiesel from used cooking oil, tailored to meet our customers' individual quality and sustainability needs. Our mission is to manufacture biodiesel in the most efficient and sustainable manner, delivering both economic and environmental benefits.

As a diesel trading company, we focus on supplying high-quality biodiesel to businesses across various industries. We leverage a vast network of accredited suppliers and factories throughout the UAE to ensure that our clients receive top-tier diesel fuel supplies.

Our commitment to exceptional customer service means we ensure prompt and safe delivery of shipments through various transportation methods. We also adhere to all local environmental standards and regulations, employing best practices to help preserve the environment and reduce pollution.

If you are seeking a trusted and reliable biodiesel supplier, contact us for more information on our services and how we can meet your business needs.

Important Features of Biodiesel

  • Biodegradable and Renewable Fuel: Biodiesel is derived from renewable resources and decomposes naturally, making it an environmentally friendly alternative to fossil fuels.
  • Safer to Use: Biodiesel has low toxicity compared to traditional diesel fuel, reducing potential health risks.
  • Lower Exhaust Emission Rate: Biodiesel produces fewer emissions compared to conventional diesel fuel, contributing to cleaner air.
  • Quality Assurance: The quality of our biodiesel is analyzed according to ASTM D 6751 parameters to ensure it meets high standards.
  • No Engine Modification Required: Using biodiesel in existing diesel engines does not require any modifications, making it a convenient alternative.
